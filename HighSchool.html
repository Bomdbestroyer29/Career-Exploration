Truck Driving




Job Summary

Truck drivers are responsible for safely transporting goods and materials across various locations. They load and unload cargo, maintain vehicles, adhere to schedules, and communicate effectively. Working long hours, including nights and weekends, they navigate local and long-haul routes, ensuring compliance with traffic laws and safety regulations. Truck driving demands skill, responsibility, and time management, offering both challenges and rewards.





Skills Needed

  • Communication. The ability to convey information
  • Organization
  • Discipline
  • Trustworthiness
  • Patience
  • TIme Management
  • Navagation Skills



Education/Requirements/Salaries

  • Highschool Diploma or Equivalent
  • CDL - Class A or Class B or Class C
  • Sometimes HAZMAT and Tanker endorsements required
  • 50k-150k



Related Jobs

  • Bus Driver
  • Forklift Operator
  • Logistics Coordinator
